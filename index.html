Legacy of Speed

Ferrari has been dominating Formula 1 since 1950, with a legacy of unparalleled engineering, innovation, and success. From the first race win to the modern-day dominance, Ferrari continues to inspire millions of fans around the world with their passion for speed and excellence. With a record number of Constructors' Championships and a deep commitment to continuous improvement, Ferrari remains the heart and soul of Formula 1 racing.
